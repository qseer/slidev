## Ecological Principles

<Theorem type="definition" title="Species Richness">

The number of different species represented in an ecological community, landscape, or region.

</Theorem>

<Theorem type="theorem" title="Species-Area Relationship">

$S = cA^z$

where $S$ is species richness, $A$ is area, and $c$, $z$ are constants.

</Theorem>

## Sampling Techniques

- Canopy fogging with pyrethrin
- Light traps at multiple heights
- Malaise traps along transects

<Block type="warning" title="Challenge">

Access to upper canopy (40-60m) required specialized climbing equipment.

</Block>

::right::

## Laboratory Analysis

- DNA barcoding (COI gene)
- Morphological taxonomy
- Phylogenetic analysis

<Block type="success" title="Result">

89% of specimens successfully barcoded and identified to species.

</Block>
