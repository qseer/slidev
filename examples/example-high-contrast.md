## WCAG Guidelines

<Theorem type="definition" title="Perceivable">

Information and user interface components must be presentable to users in ways they can perceive.

</Theorem>

<Theorem type="theorem" title="Contrast Ratio">

For WCAG AAA compliance:

$\text{Contrast Ratio} \geq 7:1$ for normal text

$\text{Contrast Ratio} \geq 4.5:1$ for large text

</Theorem>

## Inaccessible Design

- Low contrast text
- Small touch targets
- No keyboard navigation

<Block type="danger" title="Impact">

Excludes 15-20% of potential users.

</Block>

::right::

## Accessible Design

- High contrast (7:1+)
- Large interactive areas
- Full keyboard support

<Block type="success" title="Impact">

Universal access with improved UX for all.

</Block>
