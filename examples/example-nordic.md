## Nordic Model

- Universal healthcare
- Generous parental leave
- Strong social safety net

<Block type="success" title="Outcome">

Low inequality (Gini: 0.27) with high productivity.

</Block>

::right::

## Anglo-Saxon Model

- Market-based healthcare
- Limited parental leave
- Targeted social programs

<Block type="warning" title="Trade-off">

Higher inequality (Gini: 0.39) but labor flexibility.

</Block>
