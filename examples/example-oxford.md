</Theorem>

## Continental Tradition

- Phenomenological approach
- Emphasis on lived experience
- Hegel, Husserl, Heidegger

<Block type="info" title="Key Insight">

Being-in-the-world as fundamental ontological structure.

</Block>

::right::

## Analytical Tradition

- Logical precision
- Language analysis
- Russell, Wittgenstein, Quine

<Block type="success" title="Key Insight">

Meaning as determined by verification conditions.

</Block>
