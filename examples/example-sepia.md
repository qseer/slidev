## Literary Concepts

<Theorem type="definition" title="Stream of Consciousness">

A narrative mode that attempts to depict the multitudinous thoughts and feelings which pass through the mind.

</Theorem>

<Theorem type="theorem" title="Narrative Fragmentation">

The disruption of linear chronology reflects the modernist understanding of subjective, discontinuous temporal experience.

</Theorem>

## Traditional Novel

- Omniscient narrator
- Chronological plot
- Clear resolution

<Block type="info" title="Example">

Dickens: linear, moralistic narrative structure.

</Block>

::right::

## Modernist Novel

- Multiple perspectives
- Non-linear time
- Open endings

<Block type="success" title="Example">

Woolf: fluid consciousness, moments of being.

</Block>
