## Theoretical Foundations

<Theorem type="definition" title="Comparative Advantage">

A country has comparative advantage in producing a good if its opportunity cost of producing that good is lower than in other countries.

</Theorem>

<Theorem type="theorem" title="Heckscher-Ohlin Model">

Countries export goods that intensively use their abundant factors of production.

</Theorem>
